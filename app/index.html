About me

Hello,

I am Cristian, a passionate full stack software developer and 3D artist from Berlin, Germany. I have an engineering degree in Automatics and Computers but I am also self-taught as i am constantly exploring new ideas and technologies.

I am experienced with full stack web development mainly using Rails and AngularJS on Linux platforms. I am able to provide software consultancy and development writing well-structured code that follows design patterns and SOLID principles. I am a dedicated team member, persistent in learning and evolving with the team and keen to pick up and use new languages, tools and emerging libraries. I am always interested in understanding the whole tech stack and I am comfortable working on complicated code-bases.
